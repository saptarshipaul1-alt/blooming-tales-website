Meet your counsellor

Hi, I'm Shweta Nair

I'm a psychologist and the founder of Blooming Tales — a mental health practice built on warmth, curiosity, and a deep respect for your unique story.

My work is shaped by the belief that every person carries within them the capacity to grow. Whether you're navigating anxiety, difficult relationships, major life changes, or simply trying to understand yourself better — I'm here to walk that path with you.
