Parental Counselling

Parenting is one of the most rewarding and also one of the most emotionally demanding roles a person can hold. This space is for parents who want to understand their children better, work through their own patterns, and build more connected, conscious family relationships.

Sessions are available online and in-person, and can be held individually or with a co-parent.

  • Understanding your child's emotional and behavioural needs
  • Breaking unhelpful parenting patterns
  • Managing parental stress and burnout
  • Co-parenting communication and alignment
  • Supporting children through transitions and difficult phases
  • Building secure, emotionally safe family environments

Common questions

What to expect

Starting therapy can feel unfamiliar. Here are a few things people often ask before their first session.

How long is a session?

A standard session is 50–60 minutes. Workshops and group programs are structured separately based on your organisation's needs.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies by person and goal. We'll discuss a rough plan after your first session — some people benefit from short-term work, others prefer ongoing support.

Is everything confidential?

Yes — absolutely. Everything shared in sessions is strictly confidential, within the ethical and legal boundaries of professional practice.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes, I offer virtual sessions for individual therapy and coaching via video call for clients across India. In-person sessions are available in Hyderabad.

Not sure which service is right for you?

Just reach out — we can have a brief conversation to figure out the best fit before committing to anything.
